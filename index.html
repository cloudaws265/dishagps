📲 What RO-North Navigator Does

Plans the smartest, shortest-distance route for your day's branch visits — pick where you need to go, get the best order to visit them, and navigate straight from Google Maps. It works fully offline (only live GPS and Maps navigation need an internet connection).

☑️
1. Select branchesTick the branches you need to visit, or use "Select All" to choose everyone. Use the search box to find branches quickly.
🧭
2. Start, End & TimingChoose where you'll start from — RO Office, your current GPS location, or any specific branch. Set the end point the same way (or keep it a roundtrip). Set a start time and traffic condition for accurate arrival estimates.
🚀
3. Get your routeThe app works out the shortest total-distance order for your visits. View it as a List or a Story, see it on the mini-map, and open it directly in Google Maps for turn-by-turn navigation.
💡 Pro tips: Tap any circle on the timeline to mark that stop as visited — the progress bar updates automatically. 📖 Story mode reads your whole plan like a narrative. 📤 Share the plan to WhatsApp in one tap.
